Email Etiquette Checklist

1. Clear Subject Line
•	 Short and specific 
•	 Clearly shows purpose

2. Professional Greeting
•	 Use “Dear Sir/Ma’am” or “Dear [Name]” 
•	 Avoid “Hi bro”, “Hey” 
3. Proper Introduction
•	 Introduce yourself (name, course, purpose) 
•	 Be clear why you are writing 
 4. Polite and Respectful Language
•	 Use words like please, kindly, thank you 
•	 Be respectful and formal 
 5. Well-Structured Body
•	 Short paragraphs 
•	 Clear message 
•	 No unnecessary details 
 6. Correct Grammar and Spelling
•	 No spelling mistakes 
•	 Proper sentences 
 7. Clear Request or Purpose
•	 Mention exactly what you want
8. Professional Closing
•	 Use “Sincerely”, “Regards”  
9. Signature
•	 Your name 
•	 Roll number / contact details 
10. No Informal Language
•	 Keep it formal
