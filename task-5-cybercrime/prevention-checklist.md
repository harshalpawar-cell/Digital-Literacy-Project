Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
•	Use strong, unique passwords for each account (avoid using your name, DOB, or “123456”). 
•	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media accounts. 
•	Do not click on suspicious links in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS—even if they look official. 
•	Never share personal details (OTP, passwords, Aadhaar, bank info) with anyone online. 
•	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, shopping, or logging into sensitive accounts. 
•	Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store. 
UPI / Financial Safety Tips
•	Never approve unknown UPI collect requests (Pay requests). Scammers often trick users into “receiving” money. 
•	Do not share your UPI PIN with anyone—no bank or app will ever ask for it. 
•	Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money via UPI. 
•	Beware of fake customer care numbers found on Google—always use official websites. 
If You Become a Victim
•	Report immediately on: National Cyber Crime Portal – https://cybercrime.gov.in 
•	Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for financial fraud emergencies)
